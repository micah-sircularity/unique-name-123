Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tutoring service

How do online tutoring sessions work?

Our online tutoring sessions take place on our secure virtual classroom platform. You'll connect with your tutor via video, audio, and chat. The platform includes an interactive whiteboard, screen sharing, and file sharing capabilities to facilitate learning.

How are tutors selected and vetted?

All our tutors undergo a rigorous selection process that includes academic credential verification, background checks, and teaching demonstrations. We only accept tutors with proven expertise in their subject areas and strong communication skills.

Can I change my tutor if we're not a good fit?

Absolutely! We understand that the right match is crucial for effective learning. If you feel your current tutor isn't the right fit, you can request a change at any time, and we'll help you find a better match with no questions asked.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule a session?

We understand that schedules can change. You can cancel or reschedule a session up to 6 hours before the scheduled start time without any penalty. For late cancellations, the session may count against your monthly hours.

Is there a minimum commitment period?

No, there's no minimum commitment period. All our plans are month-to-month, and you can cancel anytime. We're confident in our service quality and believe you'll want to stay because of the results you see, not because of a contract.

How do I track my child's progress?

Parents receive detailed progress reports after each session, highlighting what was covered, areas of strength, challenges, and recommended focus areas. Additionally, our Standard and Premium plans include access to our progress tracking dashboard with detailed analytics.
